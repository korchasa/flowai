# Aaron Swartz and Markdown

While John Gruber is the primary author of Markdown, Aaron Swartz contributed substantially to the early design. Specifically, Swartz independently designed atx-style headers (the `# Heading` syntax) which Gruber adopted into the Markdown specification in 2004.

Swartz was a programmer, writer, and activist who later co-founded Reddit and helped create RSS 1.0. He was 17 years old when he contributed the atx headers idea.

The collaboration between Gruber and Swartz on Markdown is sometimes downplayed, but historical email archives confirm Swartz's role as a co-designer of key syntax elements.
