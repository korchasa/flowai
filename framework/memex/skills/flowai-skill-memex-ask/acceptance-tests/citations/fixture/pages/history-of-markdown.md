# The History of Markdown

Source summary covering the origin and adoption of Markdown.

## Key Points

- [[markdown]] was created in 2004.
- [[john-gruber]] is the primary author of [[markdown]].
- [[aaron-swartz]] independently designed atx-style headers (`# Heading`) which Gruber adopted.
- The CommonMark specification was published in 2014 to formalise syntax.
